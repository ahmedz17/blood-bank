How It Works

Step 1: Register

Register as a donor or a patient in the system to get started.

Step 2: Make a Donation Request

If you're a patient, submit a blood request. If you're a donor, submit a donation offer.

Step 3: Admin Approval

Admin will review and approve donations and requests, ensuring blood is available when needed.
